The Best Movies According to Mark

My top 3 movies of all time.

Die Hard

I like Christmas and this movie allows me to watch a cracking action movie with the excuse of watching a Christmas movie.

Predator

Fantastic action movie. It comes with all the cheesy lines and I love it.

Aliens

There is probably a theme here but no list of action movies would be complete without this masterpiece staring Sigourney Weaver.
